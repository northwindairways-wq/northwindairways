Passenger Agreement

By booking and boarding a flight with Northwind Air™, passengers agree to the following terms and conditions. These policies are designed to ensure safety, fairness, and a positive travel experience for all guests.

General Conduct

  • Passengers must follow all crew instructions at all times.
  • Disruptive, abusive, or threatening behavior is strictly prohibited.
  • Smoking and vaping are not permitted on any aircraft.
  • Alcohol consumption is limited to beverages served by crew.

Baggage Policy

  • Carry-on: 1 personal item + 1 standard carry-on (max 22x14x9 inches)
  • Checked: Up to 2 bags (max 50 lbs each); fees may apply
  • Oversized or overweight items may incur additional charges
  • Prohibited items include flammable materials, weapons, and unsealed liquids

Cancellation & Refunds

  • Tickets may be canceled within 24 hours of purchase for a full refund
  • After 24 hours, cancellation fees may apply based on fare class
  • Refunds are processed within 7–10 business days
  • Non-refundable fares may be eligible for travel credit

Special Services

  • Passengers requiring assistance must notify Northwind Air™ at least 48 hours prior to departure
  • Dietary requests, mobility support, and medical accommodations are available
  • Service animals are permitted with proper documentation
