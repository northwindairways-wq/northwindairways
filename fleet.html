Our Fleet

Northwind Air™ operates a modern, fuel-efficient fleet built around the Airbus A320neo family. These aircraft offer quiet cabins, reduced emissions, and exceptional range—perfect for connecting Pittsburgh to the world.

A320neo

  • Role: Core domestic and short-haul international
  • Range: ~3,400 nautical miles
  • Capacity: ~180 passengers
  • Routes: KPIT to JFK, ORD, YYZ, ATL

A321neo

  • Role: High-capacity domestic and transatlantic
  • Range: ~4,000 nautical miles
  • Capacity: ~200 passengers
  • Routes: KPIT to LAX, SFO, BOS, LHR

A321XLR

  • Role: Long-haul international
  • Range: ~4,700 nautical miles
  • Capacity: ~220 passengers
  • Routes: KPIT to Paris, Frankfurt, São Paulo, Tokyo

Note: The A321XLR is currently in rollout phase and will enter service in late 2026.

Why We Fly the Neo

Our commitment to sustainability and passenger comfort led us to choose the Airbus A320neo family. These aircraft offer:

  • 15–20% fuel savings over previous models
  • Quieter cabins and reduced noise pollution
  • Lower carbon emissions per seat
  • Flexible range for both domestic and international service
