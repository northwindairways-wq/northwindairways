Brand Usage Guidelines

  • Maintain clear space around the logo equal to its height.
  • Use only approved colors and fonts in branded materials.
  • Include the ™ symbol when referencing Northwind Air™ in print or digital media.
  • Do not use branding in political, controversial, or unrelated commercial contexts.
