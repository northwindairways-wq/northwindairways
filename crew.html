Our Crew

Northwind Air™ is proud to operate with a dedicated team of pilots, flight attendants, and ground staff who embody professionalism, safety, and Pittsburgh pride. Our crew is trained to deliver a smooth and welcoming experience on every flight.

Uniform & Culture

  • Modern navy-and-sky-blue uniform with Northwind insignia
  • Emphasis on clarity, courtesy, and calm under pressure
  • Training includes FAA standards, hospitality, and emergency response

Training & Safety

  • All crew certified under FAA Part 121 standards
  • Regular safety drills and simulator sessions
  • Cabin crew trained in CPR, evacuation, and passenger support
