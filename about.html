About Northwind Air™

Northwind Air™ is a Pittsburgh-based international airline founded with a clear mission: to connect the world through reliable, modern air travel rooted in regional pride. Our hub at KPIT (Pittsburgh International Airport) positions us to serve both domestic and global destinations with efficiency and purpose.

Our Story

Northwind Air™ was born from the evolution of Falconix Air—a bold startup concept focused on luxury experimentation. Under the leadership of founder Luke Schmidt, the brand transitioned into a scalable, mainline carrier with a legacy-inspired identity. Today, Northwind Air™ operates a modern fleet, offers structured cabin classes, and reflects the values of clarity, authenticity, and operational excellence.

Our Values

  • Reliability: We prioritize consistent service and dependable operations.
  • Clarity: Our branding, booking, and onboard experience are designed for simplicity and transparency.
  • Regional Pride: As a KPIT-based airline, we celebrate Pittsburgh’s legacy and future.
  • Scalability: Our infrastructure is built to grow—modular, efficient, and future-ready.

Our Vision

Northwind Air™ aims to become a globally recognized carrier with deep roots in Pennsylvania. We believe in building an airline that reflects the strength of its origin and the ambition of its founder. From KPIT to the world—this is Northwind.
